Introduction


Welcome to our research website

The mushroom farming industry has grown as a result of the notable increase in demand for mushrooms in the worldwide food market. This cultivation method is not without its difficulties, though, as it can face a number of obstacles that could compromise the quality and quantity of the final product. For example, the growth of mushrooms depends critically on environmental circumstances, which call for careful control of elements like light, humidity, and temperature. Pests, molds, and bacteria are examples of contaminants that make cultivation even more difficult and can seriously jeopardize harvests if they are not quickly controlled. Furthermore, mushroom growers are under a great deal of pressure to anticipate changes in the market and modify their cultivation practices in response, given how dynamic the needs are. Regretfully, the sector encounters considerable challenges in accurately forecasting these factors and detecting any contamination throughout the cultivation phases.

Here comes "MushroomMatrix," an intelligent web application that uses cutting-edge technologies like blockchain, image processing, and machine learning to address these issues. The application gives mushroom producers yield forecasts, demand projections, and contamination alarms by integrating various technologies. Based on the machine learning algorithm random forest, the yield prediction model takes into account a range of environmental parameters, enabling growers to make well-informed decisions to optimize their output. In the meantime, crop losses are decreased and quality is maintained thanks to the image processing module's assistance in the early detection of contamination. Because of the critical function that blockchain technology plays in guaranteeing data openness and integrity, "MushroomMatrix" serves as both an optimization tool and a means of maintaining accountability and trust throughout the supply chain.

Literature Survey




Because of its sustainability and potential to improve food security, mushroom production has become a promising area in the quickly changing realm of agriculture. According to the literature, technical developments have greatly aided in the improvement of mushroom farming techniques, increasing production and quality. Since machine learning (ML) models are being used more and more to optimize yield predictions, they are especially pertinent in this context. Numerous research have shown how well machine learning (ML) techniques, such as random forests, support vector machines, and linear regression, anticipate mushroom harvests based on extensive agricultural and environmental data.

In the meantime, image processing methods are now crucial for identifying contamination in mushroom farming, preventing any losses, and guaranteeing constant quality. The usefulness of computer vision and deep learning models in picture analysis to detect mold and other contaminants early has been highlighted in recent research. For example, timely contamination detection was greatly facilitated by a low-cost IoT ecosystem that used image processing for oyster mushroom development [4]. This demonstrates how image processing and IoT are collaborating to develop smarter farming methods. Furthermore, these technologies go beyond simple identification because they make it easier to automate quality control procedures, which is essential for increasing production volume and preserving batch uniformity. When taken as a whole, these developments highlight how crucial image processing is to reducing the danger of contamination and, eventually, improving the efficiency and dependability.

Another ground-breaking invention that has been the focus of recent literature is blockchain technology, which has revolutionary benefits for data management in mushroom farming. Traceability along the supply chain is significantly improved by the use of blockchain technology, which guarantee safe, transparent, and unchangeable records of all agricultural operations and transactions [3]. By carefully recording each step of the mushroom production process, this technology not only emphasizes how crucial it is to preserve consumer trust but also improves food safety. Blockchain serves as a vital enabler in guaranteeing the constant quality of mushrooms by providing unmatched insights into input sources like spawn and substrates. Additionally, the use of blockchain by the cultivation sector reflects a larger movement toward digitalization and data-driven farm management, bringing agricultural methods into line with contemporary technology norms.

Research Gap




Yield prediction

While machine learning models for yield prediction have shown promise, there remains a significant gap in refining these models to account for a broader range of environmental and cultivation variables. Many existing models, including the random forest algorithm used in MushroomMatrix, primarily focus on standard parameters such as temperature and humidity. However, there is a need to include the same factors in the enviorenment(outside the growshed) to enhance prediction accuracy.

Research Problem & Solution




Research Problem

Despite its expanding importance in the global food sector, mushroom production confronts a number of ongoing problems that prevent consistent yield and quality. These obstacles are essentially the variety of environmental conditions, vulnerability to contamination, and the unpredictability of market demand. Temperature, humidity, and light all have an impact on mushroom growth, needing exact management to maximize yields. Furthermore, contamination from molds, pests, and bacteria can severely impair yield, while changing market conditions necessitate farmers' adaptability and responsiveness. However, a lack of effective forecasting tools and contamination detection systems complicates the cultivation process, frequently resulting in decreased output and financial losses for growers.

Proposed Solution

In response to these issues, a suggestion is the creation of "MushroomMatrix," a full web application designed to revolutionize mushroom agriculture by combining sophisticated technologies such as machine learning, image processing, and blockchain. This novel methodology aims to give growers dependable tools for yield prediction, demand forecasting, and real-time contamination alarms. By focusing on the Sri Lankan mushroom business, the initiative is geared to solve localized difficulties while providing a model that might potentially be applied globally. The prpopsed solution, therefore, is to design a system that not only improves yield predictions through sophisticated machine learning models, but also provides a robust framework for contamination detection and data integrity, ultimately contributing to increased efficiency and sustainability in mushroom farming practices.

Research Objectives




Main Objective

The primary goal of this project is to design and develop an adaptive traffic light management system tailored for urban areas. This comprehensive system aims to enhance urban traffic control, ensuring the efficient and safe flow of vehicles and pedestrians through intelligent, real-time analysis and decision-making.




Data Collection and Analysis using CCTV Camera

One of the key sub-objectives is to gather and analyze real-time vehicle traffic data. This task involves the strategic deployment of CCTV cameras at critical urban intersections. These cameras will capture traffic patterns, vehicle density, and the presence of pedestrians, forming the basis for informed traffic management decisions.




Improve Traffic Control Efficiency using Google Traffic Services

To optimize traffic control, the system will employ intelligent traffic density analysis, utilizing data from Google Maps and weather services. By amalgamating traffic conditions, road congestion, and real-time weather information, the system will dynamically adjust traffic light timings for improved traffic flow, especially during adverse conditions.




Emergency Vehicle Prioritization

The system will include a feature dedicated to the real-time detection of emergency vehicles. Using advanced computer vision techniques, it will swiftly identify approaching emergency vehicles and facilitate their unimpeded passage through the intersection by dynamically adjusting traffic light signals.




Pedestrian Safety

Enhancing road safety and ensuring the well-being of visually impaired pedestrians is another critical aspect of this system. It will employ computer vision to detect pedestrians and potential hazards in and around crosswalks. By identifying pedestrians and potential dangers, the system can adjust traffic lights, provide auditory signals, or alert pedestrians, making urban roadways safer for everyone.




Metho dology




The proposed traffic control system incorporates a comprehensive approach integrating four key components to efficiently manage traffic and enhance road safety. First, it employs CCTV camera video footage to analyze vehicle density at intersections, utilizing YOLOv5 object detection to capture details such as vehicle count, density, traffic length, and speed. This information is then processed on a server, enabling real-time assessment of traffic conditions. Concurrently, the system integrates with Google Maps API Services to intelligently analyze traffic density, collecting data on traffic duration, ratios, speeds, and vehicle counts. Machine learning algorithms are applied to develop a traffic count prediction model, enhancing the accuracy of real-time traffic data. Additionally, the system features a real-time emergency vehicle detection module, utilizing YOLOv5 object detection to identify emergency vehicles and dynamically adjust traffic signals. The integration of IoT components and sensors optimizes signal timings, creating a smooth "green wave" for vehicles and reducing congestion. Lastly, the system focuses on pedestrian safety by employing high-resolution cameras and state-of-the-art image recognition algorithms to detect visually impaired pedestrians. The Smart Crosswalk System, integrated with the Adaptive Traffic Light Management System, ensures extended crossing times or temporary halts in vehicular traffic to prioritize the safe passage of differently abled individuals. This comprehensive methodology improves traffic management efficiency, enhances pedestrian safety, and optimizes the handling of emergency vehicles, collectively contributing to a safer and more efficient urban traffic environment.
